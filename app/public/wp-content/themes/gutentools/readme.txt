== Description ==

Welcome to Gutentools, a refined full-site editing theme designed for businesses, consulting firms, law practices, and educational institutions. Merging contemporary design with versatile functionality, Gutentools empowers you to effortlessly customize every element of your website. It offers seamless integration with WooCommerce for e-commerce, WPML for multilingual capabilities, and Contact Form 7 for efficient communication. Whether you're launching a consultancy, a legal practice, or an educational platform, Gutentools provides the flexibility and professional templates needed to create a compelling online presenceâ€”all optimized for SEO to boost your visibility.
